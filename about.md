## About VX Player

**VX Player** is a free, powerful HD video player for Android developed by **CGC-Infotech**. Our goal is simple: let you play *any* video, *anywhere*, with zero friction.

Whether you're watching a downloaded movie in MKV, streaming a live IPTV channel via M3U8, or listening to a lecture with your screen off — VX Player handles it all smoothly on every Android phone and tablet.

### Why VX Player?

- **Universal format support** — MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, WMV, WebM, H.265/HEVC, H.264, 3GP and more.
- **4K Ultra HD** — Hardware-accelerated decoding for stutter-free playback, even on budget devices.
- **Smart features** — Resume playback, autoplay, variable speed (0.25x–4x), screen lock, and per-session memory for brightness, speed & aspect ratio.
- **No ads blocking your video** — Clean, distraction-free player UI.

### About CGC-Infotech

CGC-Infotech is an Android app development studio focused on building fast, user-friendly utility apps. Our portfolio includes VX Player, File Manager, and EMI Master.
