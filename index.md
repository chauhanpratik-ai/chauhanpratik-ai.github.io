### What is VX Player?

**VX Player** is an all-format Android media player built for speed, simplicity, and a premium viewing experience. Play MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, WMV, WebM, H.265/HEVC, and stunning **4K Ultra HD** files — with zero conversion and zero hassle.

**Highlights at a glance:**

- 🎬 All-format video playback (MP4, MKV, AVI, 4K, and more)
- 📺 Cast to TV via Chromecast
- 🎧 Background audio playback with lock-screen controls
- 📱 Picture-in-Picture floating window
- 👌 Swipe gesture controls for brightness, volume & seek
- 📁 Built-in video file manager with playlists & favourites
- 🎵 Equalizer & bass boost
- 🌐 Network stream & IPTV (M3U/M3U8) support
- 🔤 External subtitle support
- ⚡ Hardware-accelerated 4K decoding
