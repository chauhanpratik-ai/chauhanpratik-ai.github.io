## ⚙️ How to Change Playback Speed in VX Player

### Method 1: From Player Controls

1. Play any video in VX Player
2. Tap the screen to show the controls overlay
3. Tap the **⚙️ Settings** or **Speed** icon
4. Select your desired speed from the list
5. Playback immediately adjusts — no restart needed

### Method 2: Long-Press Fast Forward (Quick 1.5x)

1. During playback, **long-press the right side** of the screen
2. Playback automatically jumps to 1.5x speed while your finger is held
3. Release to return to normal speed

This is ideal for quickly scanning through a slow section without opening a menu.

## 🎵 Audio Pitch Correction — Voices Stay Natural

One of the main reasons speed control fails in basic video players is pitch distortion. At 2x speed without correction, voices sound like chipmunks. At 0.5x they sound like a slowed record.

VX Player applies **automatic pitch correction** at all non-standard speeds:

| Speed | Without Correction | With VX Player Pitch Correction |
|---|---|---|
| 0.25x | Very deep, distorted | Natural, just slowed |
| 0.5x | Lower pitch | Clear, understandable |
| 0.75x | Slightly low | Natural speech |
| 1.5x | Slightly high | Natural speech |
| 2.0x | High, chipmunk | Clear, natural |

Pitch correction means you can genuinely listen to lectures at 1.75x and still understand every word clearly.

## 🎓 Speed Control for Learning

### Language Learning (0.5x–0.75x)
Slow down native-speed dialogue to catch pronunciation, intonation, and connected speech patterns. At 0.75x, natural speech becomes clearly parseable without being unnaturally slow.

### Online Courses and Lectures (1.5x–1.75x)
Most lecture content is recorded at a conversational pace that feels slow when you're focused. 1.5x gets through an hour lecture in 40 minutes. 1.75x in 34 minutes — with full comprehension for familiar material.

### Tutorial Videos (0.5x–0.75x)
Slow down hands-on tutorials to follow along with complex steps. Coding tutorials, cooking, art, and craft videos all benefit from half-speed viewing.

### Music and Dance (0.25x–0.5x)
Analyse technique in musical performances, sports, or dance at quarter or half speed. Individual movements, finger positions, and footwork become clearly visible.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What playback speeds does VX Player support on Android?
VX Player supports speeds from 0.25x (quarter speed) to 2.0x (double speed), with increments at 0.25x, 0.5x, 0.75x, 1.0x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 1.75x, and 2.0x. All speeds include automatic audio pitch correction to keep voices sounding natural.

### 2. Does changing playback speed distort audio quality in VX Player?
No. VX Player applies automatic pitch correction at all non-standard speeds, so voices and audio remain natural-sounding whether you're at 0.5x slow motion or 2x fast forward. There is no chipmunk effect at high speeds or unnatural deepening at low speeds.

### 3. How do I quickly speed up a video in VX Player without opening menus?
Long-press the right side of the screen during playback. VX Player temporarily switches to 1.5x speed while your finger is held down, then returns to normal speed when you release. This is perfect for quickly scanning through slow sections.

### 4. Can I use slow motion to analyse sports footage in VX Player?
Yes. VX Player's 0.25x and 0.5x playback speeds work with any video file and are excellent for sports analysis. Combined with frame-by-frame stepping via the seek controls, you can analyse individual frames in detail.

### 5. Does VX Player remember my playback speed setting between videos?
VX Player retains the last used speed within a session. For a persistent default speed that applies to every video you open, check Settings → Player → Default Playback Speed to set your preferred rate.
