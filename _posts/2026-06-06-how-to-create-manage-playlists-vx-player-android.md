Binge-watching a series, working through a video course, or queuing up your music videos for a party — playlists make continuous video playback effortless. **VX Player's playlist system** lets you group any combination of local files, folders, and network streams into organised queues that play automatically from start to finish.

---

## 📋 Why Use Playlists in VX Player?

Without playlists, you're tapping "next episode" manually after every video. With a playlist set up once:

- Videos **play back-to-back automatically**
- Shuffle mode randomises your queue
- Loop mode repeats the playlist endlessly
- You can **reorder, add, or remove** items without rebuilding from scratch

| Use Case | Playlist Benefit |
|---|---|
| TV series (multiple episodes) | Auto-advance, remember position |
| Music videos / concerts | Loop + shuffle |
| Video courses / tutorials | Ordered playback, resume support |
| Party / ambient video | Loop playlist all night |

## ➕ Creating a New Playlist

**Method 1 — From the Library**

1. Open VX Player and go to the **Library** tab
2. Long-press any video file
3. Tap **Add to Playlist** from the context menu
4. Choose **New Playlist** and enter a name
5. Tap **Create** — your playlist is ready

**Method 2 — From a Folder**

1. In the **Folders** view, navigate to a folder containing your episodes
2. Long-press the folder
3. Tap **Add folder to Playlist**
4. All videos in the folder are added in alphabetical/numerical order

**Method 3 — During Playback**

1. While a video is playing, tap the screen to reveal controls
2. Tap the **queue icon** (top-right area)
3. The current video's folder is loaded as a temporary queue
4. Tap **Save as Playlist** to make it permanent

---

## ✏️ Editing a Playlist

To reorder or remove items:

1. Go to **Library → Playlists**
2. Tap the playlist you want to edit
3. Tap the **pencil/edit icon**
4. **Drag handles** to reorder videos
5. Tap the **minus (−)** icon to remove a video
6. Tap **Done** when finished

---

## 🔀 Shuffle & Loop Options

Inside any playlist during playback, tap the **repeat icon** to cycle through:

| Icon state | Behaviour |
|---|---|
| Repeat off | Playlist plays once, then stops |
| Repeat all (↻) | Playlist loops continuously |
| Repeat one (↩) | Current video loops |
| Shuffle (⇌) | Random order, no repeats |

## 📥 Importing M3U Playlists

VX Player can open `.m3u` and `.m3u8` playlist files directly:

1. Tap the **network stream icon** (or use Open URL)
2. Enter the path to your M3U file: `file:///sdcard/playlists/my-list.m3u`
3. VX Player parses the file and shows all entries
4. Tap any entry to begin playback from that item

This is the same method used for IPTV streams — see the [IPTV streaming guide](/guides/2026/05/08/stream-online-videos-iptv/) for more detail.

---

## 💾 Playlist Storage & Backup

Playlists are stored locally on your device. To back them up or transfer to another phone:

1. Export playlists (Settings → Library → Export playlists) — saves as `.m3u` files
2. Copy the `.m3u` files to your new device
3. Import via Open URL / file browser on the new device
