## 🎧 What Is Background Video Playback?

Background playback means a video's audio continues even when:

- The **screen is turned off** (power button pressed)  
- You **switch to another app** (home or recent apps button)  
- The device is **locked/sleeping**  

VX Player handles both true background audio (screen off) and foreground-but-minimised (using Picture-in-Picture). This guide focuses on the screen-off background audio use case.

| Use Case | Perfect For |
|---|---|
| Screen off, audio playing | Podcasts, lectures, music videos, language learning |
| Minimised to PiP | Sports, tutorials, cooking videos |
| Locked screen controls | Commuting, exercising, driving |

## ⚙️ How to Enable Background Playback in VX Player

### Step 1: Enable in VX Player Settings

1. Open VX Player  
2. Tap the **⚙️ Settings** icon  
3. Navigate to **Player Settings**  
4. Find **Background Playback** or **Play in Background**  
5. Toggle it **ON**  

### Step 2: Grant Background Activity Permission (Android)

For background playback to survive when you switch apps or lock the screen, Android must allow VX Player to run in the background:

1. Go to **Android Settings → Apps → VX Player**  
2. Tap **Battery**  
3. Set to **Unrestricted** (or "Allow background activity")  

> 💡 On Samsung devices, look under **Settings → Device Care → Battery → Background usage limits** and exclude VX Player. On Xiaomi/MIUI, go to **Settings → Apps → Manage apps → VX Player → Battery saver** and set to **No restrictions**.

## 🔋 Battery Saving: The Real Power of Screen-Off Playback

The screen is consistently the largest battery consumer on any smartphone — often accounting for 40–60% of total battery drain during active use. By enabling background playback and turning off the screen:

| Mode | Battery Drain Rate |
|---|---|
| Full screen video playback (100% brightness) | Very high |
| Reduced brightness + video | High |
| PiP mode (video + other apps) | Moderate |
| **Background audio (screen off)** | **Low — up to 3–5× longer session** |

For a two-hour documentary or lecture, screen-off background audio can extend your battery from "45 minutes left" to "well over 3 hours" — because you're only running the CPU decoder and speaker, not illuminating millions of display pixels.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting: Background Playback Stops Unexpectedly

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Audio stops when screen turns off | Enable background activity in Android battery settings |
| Stops after ~5 minutes | Aggressive battery optimisation — set VX Player to "Unrestricted" battery mode |
| Stops when switching apps | MIUI/Samsung aggressive memory management — whitelist VX Player |
| Audio stops after a phone call | This is normal; resume VX Player after the call ends |
| Notification disappears | Ensure VX Player notification permission is enabled in Android settings |

### OEM-Specific Fixes

**Samsung:** Settings → Battery and device care → Battery → Background usage limits → Never sleeping apps → Add VX Player  

**Xiaomi/MIUI:** Settings → Apps → Manage apps → VX Player → Autostart: ON  

**Huawei/EMUI:** Phone Manager → Protected apps → Enable for VX Player  

**Oppo/ColorOS:** Settings → Battery → App Quick Freeze → Exclude VX Player

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Does VX Player support background audio playback with the screen off?
Yes. VX Player includes a background playback feature that keeps audio running after you press the power button or switch to another app. Enable it in VX Player Settings → Player Settings → Background Playback.

### 2. Why does background playback stop after a few minutes on my phone?
This is caused by Android's battery optimisation aggressively killing background apps. Go to Settings → Apps → VX Player → Battery and set it to "Unrestricted" or "No restrictions". Samsung users should also add VX Player to the "Never sleeping apps" list.

### 3. Can I use lock-screen controls with VX Player background playback?
Yes. When VX Player is playing in the background, standard Android media controls appear on the lock screen and in the notification shade. You can play, pause, and seek without unlocking your device.

### 4. Does background playback work with IPTV streams in VX Player?
Yes. Background audio works with all content types — local video files, network streams, and IPTV channels. The audio stream continues playing with the screen off, and the notification bar shows the stream title with playback controls.

### 5. Is there a sleep timer in VX Player to stop background audio automatically?
Yes. VX Player includes a sleep timer accessible from the player controls. You can set it to stop playback after 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes, or at the end of the current file — ideal for falling asleep to audio content.
