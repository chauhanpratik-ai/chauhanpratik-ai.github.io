VX Player isn't just for local files — its powerful **Network Stream & URL Player** lets you watch online videos, live streams, and IPTV channels directly inside the app. No third-party browser, no extra streaming app needed.

## Supported Protocols & Formats

VX Player supports the following streaming protocols:

| Protocol | Use Case |
|----------|----------|
| HTTP / HTTPS | Direct video URLs, hosted MP4/MKV files |
| RTSP | IP cameras, surveillance streams |
| RTMP | Live broadcasts, streaming servers |
| MMS | Microsoft Media Server streams |
| M3U / M3U8 | IPTV playlists, HLS live streams |

## How to Play a Video by URL

1. Open VX Player and tap the **Network Stream** icon (globe icon) in the top bar or from the main menu.
2. Paste or type the direct URL of the video or stream.
3. Tap **Play** — VX Player connects and starts playback.

That's it. You can use any publicly accessible direct video link (e.g. `https://example.com/video.mp4`) or a live stream URL.

## How to Load an IPTV Playlist (M3U/M3U8)

1. Obtain your M3U or M3U8 playlist URL from your IPTV provider.
2. Open VX Player → **Network Stream**.
3. Paste the playlist URL (ending in `.m3u` or `.m3u8`).
4. Tap **Play** — VX Player will load all channels from the playlist.
5. Use the channel list to browse and select a channel.

> **Tip:** Save your IPTV URL as a favourite so you can access it quickly next time without re-entering the address.

## Playing Local M3U Files

If you have an M3U playlist file downloaded to your phone or SD card:

1. Open the **File Manager** in VX Player.
2. Browse to the folder containing the `.m3u` file.
3. Tap it — VX Player automatically loads all playlist entries.

## Troubleshooting Stream Playback

**The stream won't load:**
- Check that the URL is correct and accessible from your device's browser.
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection (Wi-Fi recommended for HD streams).
- Some streams require specific headers or authentication that may not be supported.

**Video buffers constantly:**
- Try switching from Wi-Fi to mobile data or vice versa.
- Reduce the quality/resolution if the stream offers multiple options.
- For RTMP streams, ensure the stream is currently live and broadcasting.

**No audio on stream:**
- Check the audio track selector in the VX Player playback controls (speaker icon).
- Some streams have multiple audio tracks — try switching to a different one.
