We're thrilled to introduce **VX Player**, a free, feature-packed HD video player for Android built by CGC-Infotech. Whether you have a flagship phone or a budget device, VX Player is designed to deliver a smooth, premium viewing experience every time.

## Play Any Video, Instantly

Tired of "unsupported format" errors? VX Player supports every major video format out of the box:

| Format | Supported |
|--------|-----------|
| MP4    | ✅ |
| MKV    | ✅ |
| AVI    | ✅ |
| MOV    | ✅ |
| FLV    | ✅ |
| WMV    | ✅ |
| WebM   | ✅ |
| H.265 / HEVC | ✅ |
| 4K Ultra HD  | ✅ |

Just tap any video file — VX Player plays it instantly, no conversion needed.

## Hardware-Accelerated 4K Playback

VX Player uses hardware-accelerated video decoding to ensure smooth, stutter-free 4K Ultra HD playback — even on older and budget Android devices. Smart power optimization also keeps your battery running longer during extended movie sessions.

## Background Playback & Lock-Screen Controls

Need to keep listening while you check your messages? Enable **background playback** to keep audio running when the screen turns off or when you switch to another app. Full pause, seek, and skip controls appear in the notification bar and on the lock screen — perfect for music videos, podcasts, and online lectures.

## Picture-in-Picture (Floating Window)

With **PiP mode**, you can watch any video in a resizable floating popup window while browsing, chatting, or using other apps. Auto-PiP activates automatically when you leave the player, so you never lose your place.

## Smart File Manager Built In

VX Player automatically scans your phone and SD card for all video files. Browse by folder or list view, sort by name, date, size, or duration. You can even rename or delete files directly from the app, and save your favourites to a personal collection.
