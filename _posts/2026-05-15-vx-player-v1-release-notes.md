We're excited to announce the **official v1.0 release** of VX Player on the Google Play Store! This is the first public release, and it's packed with everything you need for a great video watching experience on Android.

---

## What's New in v1.0

### 🎬 Video Playback Engine
- Universal format support: MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, WMV, WebM, 3GP, H.264, H.265/HEVC
- 4K Ultra HD playback with hardware-accelerated decoding
- Smooth, stutter-free performance on budget and flagship devices
- Multiple aspect ratio modes: fit, fill, stretch, zoom

### 📺 Cast to TV
- Chromecast / Google Cast support
- Stream local videos wirelessly to your TV or any Cast-enabled device
- Zero setup required

### 🎧 Background Playback
- Audio continues when screen turns off or you switch apps
- Full playback controls in the notification bar and lock screen
- Ideal for music videos, podcasts, and lectures

### 📱 Picture-in-Picture (Floating Window)
- Resizable floating video popup while using other apps
- Auto-PiP mode activates automatically when leaving the player
- Supports drag to reposition and tap to return to full screen

### 👌 Gesture Controls
- Left-side vertical swipe: brightness
- Right-side vertical swipe: volume
- Horizontal swipe: seek
- Pinch to zoom
- Double-tap left/right to rewind/fast-forward

### 📁 Video File Manager
- Auto-scan phone and SD card for all video files
- Browse by folder or list view
- Sort by name, date, size, or duration
- Rename and delete files from within the app
- Custom playlists and Favourites collection

### 🎵 Audio Controls
- Built-in equalizer with multiple presets
- Manual per-band frequency adjustment
- Bass boost for deeper, richer sound

### 🌐 Network Streaming
- Play online videos and live streams by URL
- Supported protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, RTSP, RTMP, MMS
- M3U / M3U8 playlist support for IPTV

### 📸 Video Screenshot
- Capture any frame from a video with a single tap
- Screenshots saved directly to your gallery

### 🔤 Subtitle Support
- Load external subtitle files (.srt, .ass, .ssa)
- Select preferred subtitle language
- Select preferred audio track
- VX Player remembers your choices per video

### ⏯️ Smart Playback Controls
- Resume playback from where you left off
- Autoplay next video in folder or playlist
- Variable playback speed: 0.25x, 0.5x, 0.75x, 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 4x
- Screen and controls lock
- Customisable seek intervals: 5s, 10s, 15s, 30s
- Auto-rotate with manual orientation lock
- Per-session memory for brightness, speed, and aspect ratio

### 🌙 Themes & Display
- Dark mode for comfortable low-light viewing
- Multiple colour themes to personalise the player

---

## Known Issues

- Certain DRM-protected streams may not play (by design — DRM content requires a licensed decoder).
- Very large M3U playlists (5000+ channels) may take a few seconds to load on older devices.
- Subtitle font size customisation is coming in a future update.
